Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy and this policy explains how I collect and treat any information you give me. You won’t find any complicated legal terms or long passages of unreadable text. I've no desire to trick you into agreeing to something you might later regret.

My policy covers

  • Why I value your privacy
  • What information I hold
  • Where I store your information
  • What I use your information for
  • Who’s responsible for your information
  • Who has access to information about you
  • The steps I take to keep your information private & secure

Why I value your privacy

I value your privacy as much as I do my own, so I'm committed to keeping your personal and business information safe. I'm uncomfortable with the information companies, governments, and other organisations data-mine and keep on file, so I ask for only the bare minimum from my customers. I’ll never use your personal information for any reason other than why you gave it, and I’ll never give anyone access to it.

Zero tracking

My website is static, secure (confirmed by the padlock icon in the browser address bar) and does not use cookies or scripts that were designed to detect, register or track you or your activity. I don't engage in any form of tracking or analysis of site visitor data and I don’t use analytics or native social media ‘like’ or ‘sharing’ buttons which also build profiles of your internet activity.

What information I hold

When you contact me by email or through my website, I collect your name and email address. If you do business with me, I also collect your business name and phone number and keep records of the invoices I send you and the payments you make.

Where I store your information

If we do business, I store your information offline in my accounts software package. I chose this package partly due to the developers commitment to security.

What I use your information for

I will use your information to send you invoices, statements, or reminders.

Who’s responsible for your information

I (John Coyne) am responsible for the security of your information. You can contact me by phone on 0779 282 7498 or email john@iolla.net if you have any concerns about the information I store.

Who has access to information about you

When I store information in my systems, only I have access to it.

Steps I take to keep your information private & secure

Access to your information is restricted to myself only. I store passwords in an encrypted and secure password manager, use a different, randomly generated and highly complex password for each customer, and never use the same password twice. The computers I use are fully encrypted using File Vault and are protected by either passcode or fingerprint biometric access. My computers ask for my authentication whenever they are started and after 5 minutes of inactivity. My mobile device is also protected by facial recognition biometrics.

How to complain

I take complaints very seriously. If you’ve any reason to complain about the ways I handle your data or privacy, please contact me by phone 0779 282 7498 or email john@iolla.net.

Changes to the policy

If I change the contents of this policy, those changes will become effective the moment I publish them on my website.
